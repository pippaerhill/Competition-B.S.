About Me

I am Sarah Jones
Wedding, Pet and Portrait photographer

Sarah Jones is an artist, and a happily married mother of three. most days she has a camera in hand and is creating images of the delicious moments in life. her photographs graced the covers and pages of countless magazines, and continue to take her to the far corners of the world. Sarah Jones first book, THE LUMINOUS PORTRAIT, offers intimate insights to her creative process. her second book, ADORNMENTS, captures the beauty of designer myra callan's handmade flowers & such. her most recent book, THE SILVER LINING. a collaboration with her close friend hollye jacobs, a supportive, inspirational, and visually beautiful guide to breast cancer. elizabeth is happiest when she is making pictures or snuggling her sweet children. when she's not capturing other people's love stories, you will find her at home, nurturing her own or chasing after her puppy, luna. messina heart
